ABOUT GLOBAL CX SUMMIT

Global CX Summit 2019, brought to you by Exito Media Concepts, is an experiential platform curated to strengthen your customer strategy and thereby help you deliver best-in-class Customer Experience in this age of the empowered customer.

This dynamic event will provide remarkable insights and an excellent opportunity to develop sagacity in terms of CX innovation. Designed to maximize productivity, it is packed with inspiring talks and high-energy interaction sessions. Global CX Summit is a one-day initiative designed to bring together 180+ customer experience leaders and technology heads to discuss the latest trends and technology and see what it has in store for organizations.

Learn From Industry Leaders

Hear from a diverse group of thinkers and doers in a series of talks and workshops in a well-curated one-day agenda.

Sharpen your skills

Masterclasses from the masters themselves allows you to dive into new disciplines, trends, and technologies.

Broaden your horizons

Explore new ideas and methodologies around Emerging tech, Disruptive innovation, digital transformation, and more in expert-hosted breakout sessions.

Connect and Click

From coffee-breaks to networking luncheons, Global CX Summit provides you with countless opportunities to meet fellow attendees and talk about the things that truly matter.

Featured discussions

Conceived with a vision to promote a knowledge and networking platform among the CX and technology leaders, Global CX Summit will highlight the following topics. We will extensively explore topics around Omnichannel Experience, Data Centralization, The impact of Predictive analytics, IoT, and AI on CX, Balance between privacy and personalization to begin with.

CX as the Source of Competitive Advantage
Making Sense of the Mayhem (Data Centralization)
Predictive Analytics: What do customers really want?
Balancing Personalization and Privacy to gain trust
The Impact of Artificial Intelligence on CX
The Future of Transformational CX
